About Us
Limonar Mexican Restaurant is a family-owned business opened this year. We have created Limonar Mexican Restaurant to serve all time favorite Mexican dishes like fajitas, tacos and so much more! Our store is known for its variety in taste and fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service. We are accepting both dine-in and take out orders.
